Invitation Letter and Visa Application Documents Request Instructions


To help assist our authors and participants applying for invitation letter and Japanese VISA, this page will give explanations about the following key points.

  1. If you only need an English invitation letter, please download and fill in Part A of the invitation letter request form. Then, please email your request to cyberscitechcongress@gmail.com with the subject “Invitation letter request” and attached the filled request form in MS Word format, together with your registration payment confirmation from Nishitetsu Travel. An electronic invitation letter will be emailed to you.

  2. Please note, before requesting for an invitation letter, please complete your registration first. If multiple authors of one paper all plan to attend the conference and need invitation letters, all have to register accordingly (please refer to the registration instruction page: http://cyber-science.org/2019/ -> Attend -> Participant Registration), and then make separate requests by providing their request forms.

  3. If you also need to apply for a Japanese VISA, please fill in Part B of the information collection form. Two sample forms are provided below as examples to assist in filling the form.

  4. If you require additional VISA application documents and a physical copy of the invitation letter to be mailed to you, an additional charge of 4000 JPY will be charged.


If you have any questions, please e-mail them to cyberscitechcongress@gmail.com, with the full author names and paper ID (the 10 digit ID number provided by EDAS).
